      Director's Message

      Dr. Vinay Garodia

      After finishing my training and tenure in 2001 at AIIMS, New Delhi, we started our eye centre to provide the state-of-the-art treatment. In the last 20 years we have treated more than 100,000 patients and have performed more than 10,000 eye surgeries successfully.

      We value Competence. We are proud to have a team of highly qualified doctors, mostly trained at AIIMS, New Delhi and Royal College of Surgeons, England. Some of our doctors have been Professors at the leading Medical Colleges like AIIMS Delhi, MAMC and Meerut Medical College. Each of our staff has received the highest level of training and they are continuously trained on a regular basis at our centre.

      We have built institutions of Integrity, where treatment is done keeping best interest of the patient in mind. We love to educate our patients and involve them in their healthcare and decision making.

      Safety is very important to us. We are one of the first eye centres in Delhi to get NABH accreditation. We ensure complete safety for our patients, attendants, doctors and staff.

      Compassion is a part of our culture. Everyone in Team Synergy tries their best to help the patients and make them as comfortable as possible.

      We offer affordable high quality eye care. We also have a program for helping economically weaker sections of the society. I wish everyone a very happy and healthy life full of colours.



      Dr. Vinay Garodia
